Terms, Policy & How It Works
TBR Rewards — Full Terms & Policy
Effective: May 2026 · Version 1.0
What is TBR Rewards?
TBR Rewards is a legitimate reward-based earning platform built as a Telegram Mini App. Users earn real money by completing simple daily micro-tasks such as watching short advertisements and inviting friends.
We partner with advertising networks and sponsors who pay us for genuine user engagement. A significant portion of that revenue is shared directly with you. Every reward you receive is backed by real advertiser budgets — making TBR Rewards a transparent, honest earning platform.
TBR Rewards is completely free to join and use. There is no deposit, no subscription, and no hidden fee required at any point.
How to Use TBR Rewards
Step 1 — Create your account. Open TBR Rewards through Telegram. Complete the quick onboarding (gender and age confirmation) and your account is created instantly. Your Telegram identity is used securely — no password needed.
Step 2 — Watch your daily ads. Each day you are assigned a set of ad tasks. Tap the first available ad task, the ad will load on your screen, and a timer will count down. Once the timer finishes, tap "Claim Reward" and your earnings are instantly added to your balance. You must complete ads in order — the next ad unlocks only after the previous one is claimed.
Step 3 — Complete all daily tasks. Your daily tasks reset every 24 hours. Completing all ads assigned for the day maximizes your earnings for that day. Missing a day simply means you pick up from your current position the next day.
Step 4 — Invite friends. Go to the Invite tab, copy your unique referral link, and share it. Every friend who joins earns you a referral bonus automatically. You also earn from their referrals at multiple network levels.
Step 5 — Withdraw your earnings. Once your balance reaches the minimum for your chosen payment method, go to the Withdraw tab, fill in your details, and submit. All payments are processed within 7–14 business days.
Daily Earnings & Ad Tasks
TBR Rewards assigns a different number of ad tasks each day. Your daily earning potential changes from day to day — it will generally be anywhere between $2 and $10 per day depending on the day's schedule. The exact number of ads and your potential earnings for that day are clearly displayed each time you open the app.
Each ad task has a timer that runs between 10 and 30 seconds. The duration is set randomly for each ad. You must remain on the ad screen for the full timer duration before your reward is credited. Closing or switching screens during the timer will not credit your balance.
Your earning cycle operates on a repeating schedule. Your cumulative balance grows until you reach the cycle threshold, at which point the daily task schedule resets and a new earning cycle begins. This allows unlimited earning over time.

Important: Your entire balance history and progress are saved permanently. If you close the app and return days, weeks, or even months later, everything will be exactly as you left it.

Referral Program
Our multi-level referral program lets you earn passively from your entire network:
  • Level 1 — Direct: $0.50 for every friend who joins through your link and completes their first ad.
  • Level 2 — Indirect: $0.10 for every person your direct referrals bring in.
  • Level 3 — Extended: $0.05 from the third level of your network.
There is no limit to how many people you can invite. Referral bonuses are credited within 24 hours of the qualifying event. Fake or self-referrals will result in account suspension and forfeiture of all earnings.

Referral Balance Withdrawal Rule: Your referral balance is kept completely separate from your task earnings. Referral earnings can only be withdrawn once you have successfully referred a minimum of 10 users (minimum referral balance of $50). Referral balance is not counted toward your regular task withdrawal threshold.

Fair Use & Anti-Fraud Policy
  • Each ad must be watched for its full timer duration. Bypassing the timer in any way will not credit the reward.
  • Using VPNs, proxies, bots, scripts, or any automation tool is strictly prohibited and results in permanent suspension.
  • Only one account per user is allowed. Multiple accounts from the same device or IP will be banned.
  • Any attempt to manipulate balances, task records, or referral counts will result in immediate account termination and forfeiture of all earnings.
  • We use automated fraud detection. Flagged accounts are reviewed within 5 business days.
  • We reserve the right to hold a withdrawal for up to 14 business days for manual verification before processing.
Privacy Policy

We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing. Advertising partners receive only anonymous aggregate data. Financial details you provide for withdrawals are stored securely and used solely for payment processing.
Customer support conversations are automatically and permanently deleted after 7 days. You may request full account and data deletion at any time via the in-app support chat.
